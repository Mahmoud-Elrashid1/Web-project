Campus Event Hub is a platform designed to help students discover and participate in events happening across campus. It allows clubs, departments, and organizations to create and promote events while helping students easily find activities that match their interests.

Our Mission

Our goal is to make campus life more engaging by connecting students with workshops, seminars, networking events, and social activities.

How It Works

  • Students can browse upcoming events.
  • Organizations can submit new events.
  • Admins review and approve events.
  • Students can RSVP to attend.
